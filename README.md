# 🚀 Vision

Customer churn is one of the largest revenue risks for SaaS businesses, yet identifying at-risk customers often requires analysts, SQL expertise, dashboards, and multiple reporting cycles before meaningful action can begin.

**SynapKeep AI** eliminates this delay by enabling executives to ask business questions in plain English. Powered by **Google Gemini 2.5 Flash**, the platform converts natural language into validated SQL, analyzes structured customer telemetry, visualizes business risk, and generates AI-powered customer retention strategies—all within a unified executive dashboard.

The result is a dramatically shorter path from business question to business action.

# ⚙️ Engineering Decisions

SynapKeep AI separates deterministic analytics from generative reasoning.

- Natural language business questions are translated into schema-aware SQL.
- SQL is validated before execution to ensure read-only safety.
- Customer telemetry is retrieved from SQLite.
- Executive metrics are computed using deterministic analytics.
- Google Gemini generates personalized retention playbooks from structured telemetry.
- Explainable AI panels describe why SQL was generated.
- Confidence scoring provides transparent AI reliability indicators.

This architecture combines the reliability of traditional analytics with the flexibility of Generative AI.

# 🧪 Demo Workflow

### Step 1

Ask a business question in natural language.

Example:

```text
Show me customers with more than 3 support tickets and low activity.
```

---

### Step 2

Google Gemini 2.5 Flash translates the request into a schema-aware SQL query.

Example:

```sql
SELECT *
FROM customer_health
WHERE support_tickets_opened > 3
AND days_inactive > 30;
```

---

### Step 3

The generated SQL passes through the SQL Safety Validation layer.

Validation checks include:

- Read-only verification
- Schema validation
- Restricted SQL operations
- Safety confirmation

---

### Step 4

SQLite retrieves matching customer telemetry.

Returned information includes:

- Company
- Monthly Revenue
- Customer Health
- Feature Adoption
- Platform Usage
- Support Tickets
- Customer Activity

---

### Step 5

Executive dashboards automatically compute:

- Revenue at Risk
- Critical Customers
- Customer Health Distribution
- Executive KPIs
- Business Impact
- AI Confidence Scores

---

### Step 6

Select any customer and click

🚀 **Synthesize Personalized Recovery Playbook**

Gemini generates:

- Executive Summary
- Business Risks
- Immediate Actions
- Retention Strategy
- Customer Email Draft
- Expected Business Impact
